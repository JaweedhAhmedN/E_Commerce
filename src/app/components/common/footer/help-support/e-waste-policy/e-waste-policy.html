E-Waste Management Policy

Our commitment to responsible recycling and sustainable practices

1. Purpose

At {{ appName }}, we recognize the importance of environmentally responsible disposal of electronic waste (e-waste). This policy outlines our approach to managing e-waste in compliance with applicable laws and sustainability goals.

2. What is E-Waste?

E-waste refers to discarded electronic products such as mobile phones, laptops, chargers, batteries, and other devices that contain hazardous materials. Improper disposal can harm the environment and human health.

3. Our Commitment

  • Partnering with authorized e-waste recyclers for safe disposal and recycling.
  • Encouraging customers to return old electronics through our take-back program.
  • Promoting awareness about e-waste hazards and sustainable practices.

4. Customer Guidelines

We encourage our customers to:

  • Return old electronics to designated collection centers or authorized recyclers
  • Avoid disposing e-waste in regular trash bins
  • Contact our support team for assistance with e-waste disposal

5. Legal Compliance

{{ appName }} complies with the E-Waste (Management) Rules, 2016 issued by the Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change, Government of India. We ensure that all e-waste is handled by certified recyclers in accordance with regulatory standards.
